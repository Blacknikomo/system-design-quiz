🧠 System Cheat Sheets

Terse recall cards for 30 classic design problems — functional / non-functional / out-of-scope requirements, tech choices and why, the "great" moves, and an interactive architecture diagram (click a module or an arrow). Built for active recall: see the name, reconstruct the design, then reveal to check.

Tip: in Recall mode, cards stay collapsed — try to reconstruct the design out loud (including the diagram!), then click to reveal. Pairs with the quiz, the walkthroughs and the per-technology simulators.
