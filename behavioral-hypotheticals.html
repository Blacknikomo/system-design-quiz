🔀 Frameworks for Hypothetical Questions

“How would you handle… / What would you do…” A building-block library of reusable frameworks — select and combine based on the question. Diagnostic (what’s wrong?), Decision (what to choose?), Communication (how to handle?). Built for active recall — read the category, recall the frameworks, then reveal.

Adapted from “Mastering Behavioral Interviews: The Guide to Storytelling in Tech.” Pairs with the Story Map, Story Craft, Best Practices, and the quiz.
