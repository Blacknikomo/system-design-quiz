🗺️ Behavioral Story Map

For each question archetype: which STAR story to deploy, the competency it signals, the follow-ups to expect, and the trap to avoid. Built for active recall — read the archetype, recall your story, then reveal to check.

One story, many questions

Strong stories flex across archetypes. Build ~10–12 and map each to several. High-reuse anchors:

  • Underperformer turnaround → Underperformance · People Growth · Conflict
  • Senior-engineer conflict → Conflict · Influence w/o Authority · People Growth
  • Tough trade-off / said no → Ambiguity & Results · Strategy & Vision
  • A real failure → Self-Awareness · Ambiguity & Results

Tip: in Recall mode, cards stay collapsed — say which story you'd deploy and why out loud, then click to reveal. Pairs with the Story Craft and Best Practices guides and the quiz.
